About Zyra

Zyra is a habit-building app for children (ages 5–10) that turns daily routines into fun, gamified adventures. Each completed task contributes “footsteps” that help children move closer to unlocking a new object (such as a Dinosaur, a Flower, a Bird). Zyra combines gamification, AR, and AI to build healthy habits, foster learning, and strengthen the parent-child bond.
